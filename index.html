What We Do

Our Cleaning Services

Comprehensive External Cleaning solutions tailored for San Diego homes and businesses.

☀️

Solar Panel Cleaning

Dirty panels = lost energy. We safely remove dust, bird droppings, and sea salt buildup to restore maximum efficiency and protect your investment.

💡 Why It Matters & How Often

Dirty solar panels can lose 15–25% of their energy output. San Diego's coastal air deposits salt and dust quickly, silently cutting into your savings every day. Regular cleaning keeps your system running at peak performance.

🗓 Every 3–6 months
🍂

Gutter Cleaning

Clogged gutters can lead to costly water damage. We clear out leaves, debris, and blockages so water flows freely away from your home.

💡 Why It Matters & How Often

Blocked gutters force water under your roofline, rotting fascia boards and flooding foundations. Even in San Diego's dry climate, wind-blown debris and eucalyptus leaves build up fast and cause serious damage if ignored.

🗓 1–2 times per year
🪟

Window Cleaning

Streak-free, crystal-clear windows inside and out. We handle residential and commercial properties of all sizes with professional-grade equipment.

💡 Why It Matters & How Often

San Diego's ocean breeze deposits salt film and hard water minerals on glass that permanently etch the surface over time. Clean windows also flood your home with natural light and dramatically improve curb appeal.

🗓 Every 2–3 months
💦

Pressure Washing

Blast away grime, mold, oil stains, and years of buildup from driveways, decks, patios, fences, and concrete surfaces.

💡 Why It Matters & How Often

Mold, mildew, and algae make surfaces slippery and dangerous — and they silently eat away at concrete, wood, and stone. Pressure washing restores curb appeal and significantly extends the life of your outdoor surfaces.

🗓 Once or twice a year
🧼

Soft Washing

Gentle but powerful low-pressure cleaning for delicate surfaces like roofs, painted siding, stucco, and wood — safe and highly effective.

💡 Why It Matters & How Often

High-pressure water can crack roof tiles, strip paint, and force moisture behind siding causing rot. Soft washing uses eco-friendly solutions to kill mold, algae, and bacteria at the root for a deeper, longer-lasting clean without any damage.

🗓 Every 1–2 years
